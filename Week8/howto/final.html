What now?

The news ticker is just one small application of The Guardian's Open Platform API, which could be extended in a myriad of ways. You could create a ticker just for sports-related news, for example. Or you could combine it with other APIs, like those for weather or stock information, and create a full-featured ticker. Or, forget the ticker. You could use the body property included in the data for each article to create a Guardian-powered news reader. You could hook that up to the new text-to-speech API and create a machine that reads the news to you.

You get the idea. While we have scratched the surface in terms of application, the basics you've learned about APIs, keys, requests and handling machine-data will prove helpful in most situations; there are often more complications we didn't mention here, however, like OAuth, or what to do if you need commercial-level access that are a bit beyond our scope.

Good luck!
